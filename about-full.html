Our Journey

The Story Unfolds

Every great journey has pivotal moments. Here's how One Gear Garage came to be.

The Breakdown

My truck went into the shop and I lost my job. Suddenly, I was stranded with no transportation and no income. What seemed like the worst possible situation became the catalyst for everything that followed.

The First Ride

With no money to fix the truck (which is still in the shop), I bought my first pedal bike since I was a kid. It was supposed to be temporary transportation, but something magical happened the moment I started riding.

The Obsession Begins

What started as necessity became passion. I found myself learning tricks, diving deep into bike maintenance, and understanding every component. The bike wasn't just transportation anymore—it was freedom, creativity, and endless possibility.

Dreams Take Flight

Before I knew it, I was dreaming about tricks I couldn't even do yet. That bike became my training ground, my outlet, and my constant companion. Through countless miles and endless practice, I discovered the true power of human-powered machines.

One Gear Garage is Born

The realization hit me: these simple, human-powered machines are more powerful than people think. I built One Gear Garage to share that same reliability, freedom, and joy with other riders—because every bike deserves to be treated as the incredible machine it is.

The Bike That Started It All

This is the bike that changed everything. From desperate necessity to pure passion, it's been my reliable companion through countless miles and endless adventures. Every service at One Gear Garage carries the spirit of this transformative journey.
